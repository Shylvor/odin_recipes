Lasagna

Description

This delicious dish is known to be my favourite dish since I was little. This is due to is amazing flavour and my passion for pasta

Due to that love to lasagna, I've decided to show you how you all can make this amazing dish at home

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Carrots
  • Tomato
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a bowl
  2. Chop the carrots and tomatoes and mix them together
  3. Add the meat and mix for two minutes
  4. Build the lasagna up and put it on the oven
