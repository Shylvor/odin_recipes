Fried Chicken

Description

This delicious dish is know to be my brother's favourite dish since he was little.

Due to that love to fried chicken (not only by him, but also by me), I've decided to show you how you all can make this amazing dish at home

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a bowl with the chicken and the eggs
  2. Mix the filets with the bread crumbs
  3. Fry the chicken
